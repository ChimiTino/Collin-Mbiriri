Audi South Africa · 360° Campaign · Automotive

Audi
0% Deposit

No limits. No constraints. No deposit. A premium automotive campaign from concept to dealership floor.

The Brief

Sell ambition.
Not just a car.

The Audi A4, A5 and Q Range 0% deposit promotion needed to do two things simultaneously: communicate a clear financial offer, and maintain the premium aspirational quality of the Audi brand. Getting the tone wrong in either direction — too promotional, or too distant — would undermine both.

The campaign needed to reach prospective buyers across national TV, digital, social media, outdoor, and right down to the dealership experience itself — every touchpoint had to feel cohesive and premium.

Campaign Work

The work,
in full.

From dealer print to digital OOH, every execution carried the same unmistakable visual language — confident, premium, and built for impact at every scale.

Creative Approach

Every touchpoint
a statement.

The campaign line — No limits. No constraints. No deposit. — became the thread connecting every execution. It spoke to the freedom of Audi ownership while making the financial offer feel empowering rather than promotional.

The media strategy was comprehensive: national TV spots featuring the A4 and A5 were supported by YouTube pre-rolls and Google Search, Facebook Canvas and Carousel Ads, and 22 digital OOH billboards in major metropolitan areas across South Africa.

"At the dealership level, the campaign lived in giant plinths, branded coffee cup sleeves, reception decals, and customer folders. The detail mattered as much as the headline."

The Dealer Activation Toolkit extended the campaign to every franchise — window banners, waiting area table talkers, dealer print ads, TV and radio decals, and branded customer collateral — ensuring the experience was consistent from the first digital touchpoint to the moment someone signed on the dotted line.
