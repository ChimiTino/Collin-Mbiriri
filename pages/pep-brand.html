Shaping the soul of South Africa's most-loved retail brand — across every touchpoint, in every home.

Overview

More than retail.
A brand that belongs.

PEP is not just a store — it's part of the everyday fabric of South African life. For millions of families across the country, PEP is where school uniforms come from, where the first pair of shoes was bought, where home comes together week by week.

The challenge at the brand platform level is never about a single product or promotion. It's about sustaining emotional relevance at scale — making sure that every campaign, every piece of communication, every touchpoint reinforces why PEP belongs in people's lives.

Creative Direction

Warmth at scale.
Precision in execution.

Leading the creative direction on PEP's brand platform meant overseeing everything from the high-level brand narrative down to how a single retail price point is styled on a leaflet. It's the kind of work that demands both strategic clarity and hands-on design rigour.

The brand platform work spanned TV commercials, digital campaigns, social content, in-store environments, and seasonal retail campaigns — all anchored to the same warm, human brand truth: PEP is for everyone, because everyone deserves the best.

"Retail creativity at this level lives in the detail. A family on a billboard needs to feel real. A price point needs to communicate value without cheapening the brand."
Deliverables

360° across
every medium

The brand platform work included TV commercials (Keep Up The Pace, PEP Home series, Back to School, Changing Stations), digital and social campaigns across Facebook, Instagram and YouTube, influencer creative briefs and toolkits, in-store POS and signage, leaflet and print campaigns, and OOH / outdoor advertising across Southern Africa.
