Shaping the soul of South Africa's most-loved retail brand — across every touchpoint, in every home.

Overview

There's a PEP
for that.

We launched a brand platform that shifts PEP HOME from selling products to championing the surprise and delight moments our customers live for. The film shows how when life happens, PEP HOME helps — with affordable décor to refresh rooms, kitchenware for feasts, textures for comfort after long days, storage that tames chaos, and touches that turn gatherings into memories.

We make home magic accessible, connecting families and empowering South Africans to transform homes into joyful spaces. For every need, every win, every celebration, and every day in between — There's a PEP for that. And that, too.

Creative Direction

From Retail.
To Bretail.

PEP's historic focus on product and price-led advertising would reinforce rather than change perceptions. The creative challenge was to move beyond that — to complement PEP's leadership in 'lowest prices' with something more lasting.

The answer was Bretail: simple, emotive storytelling that shows a real affinity with our customers, executed at the speed of retail. Less about the shelf. More about the lives those shelves serve.

"Retail creativity at this level lives in the detail. A family on a billboard needs to feel real. A price point needs to communicate value without cheapening the brand."
Deliverables

360° across
every medium

The brand platform work included TV commercials (Keep Up The Pace, PEP Home series, Back to School, Changing Stations), digital and social campaigns across Facebook, Instagram and YouTube, influencer creative briefs and toolkits, in-store POS and signage, leaflet and print campaigns, and OOH / outdoor advertising across Southern Africa.
