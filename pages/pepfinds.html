PEP · #PEPHomeFinds · UGC / Social / Omnichannel

PEP Home
Finds

Surrendering creative control to the consumer — and winning the culture.

Overview

Give the people
the camera.

The creative strategy behind #PEPHomeFinds was to surrender creative control to the consumer — empowering real South Africans to redefine affordable aesthetics through authentic, lo-fi user-generated content showcasing their actual homes and actual budgets.

Rather than producing polished brand content, PEP stepped back and let the community lead. The result was a campaign that felt genuinely real, because it was — every frame captured on someone's phone, in someone's living room, telling their own story.

Creative Strategy

No studio.
No art direction.

Since its inception, the deliberate creative intention has always been to allow creators full creative licence to edit, title, use music and design the story on their own mobile devices. Creators were handed zero brand restrictions — they chose the music, the angles, the captions, the story. The only brief was: show us your home.

There is no cut-and-paste or repetitive component to this campaign. Every piece of content is made-to-measure for platform tone, format and personality — naturally built for TikTok, Instagram and Facebook, never converted from another marketing channel.

"There is no repetitive component to this campaign — every piece of content is as individual as the home it comes from."
Media Strategy

Entirely native.
Entirely theirs.

The campaign is entirely native to social. Every piece of content was authentically made by PEP's own customers posting to TikTok, Instagram and Facebook. Nothing was briefed into a production company or shaped in post — the community did what the community does, and the brand embraced it fully.

Community management was central to the strategy: responding, resharing and celebrating creator content kept the flywheel turning and reinforced PEP's genuine affinity with the people it serves.

Campaign Film

Closing the Loop

From scroll
to store.

To convert digital engagement into real sales, PEP integrated physical in-store POS signage to prompt social discovery in-aisle — bridging the gap between a TikTok scroll and a shop floor moment.

The launch of the "Find Stock" digital feature alongside PEP HOME e-commerce captured purchase intent at its highest point, closing the omnichannel loop and turning authentic content into measurable commercial impact.
