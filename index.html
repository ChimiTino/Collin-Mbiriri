About

Design with style.
Design with smile.

I grew up in Zimbabwe with a pen in my hand and a head full of ideas. What started as a passion for visual communication became a 20-year journey across some of Africa's most respected agencies and brands.

From the drawing boards of Dicomm McCann in Harare, to leading automotive creative at AdMakers International, to where I am today — Creative Director at Ogilvy Cape Town — every chapter has sharpened how I think, lead, and create.

I believe creativity has to do more than look good. It has to move people, change behaviour, and sometimes, save lives. The work I'm most proud of does exactly that.

"I'm always looking for real and tough challenges that can squeeze out the best of me — pushing my limits over and over again."
